How to do Laundry

Do your laundry once a week! Underwear, camisoles, and socks should be washed after each wear. Bras can be worn twice before washing. Other garments, unless specified, should be washed based on activity. For example, a tshirt worn during jogging should be washed after one wear due to sweat. Spot clean stains with soap, and if necessary, a little bleach as soon as possible.

How to Clean Dishes by Hand

To avoid a huge pile, clean your dishes after each meal . Do not forget to srub off crumbs and remains from your plate and always clean your utensils as well. Bowls or plates holding oil-free foods, such as fruit, need only a thorough rinse with water wihtout soap. Finally, empty the sink strainer when you are finished!

How to Clean the Bathroom

Use as clean as possible, wiping off any visible dust after each use to avoid build up of dirt and grime. Clear up bath strainer in case of clogging of hairs. Try to clean the bathroom once a week.

How to Organize your Desk

Throw away any unnecessary papers before placing on desk. Try to organize as you use and wipe off once a week.

How to Pay Bills

Remember to pay your bills on time to avoid fees and record all transactions in a mini notebook to keep track. Be aware of online fraud when paying online and speak with your bank immediately for any suspicious projects you did not make. Be thrifty and be debtless!

How to Buy Groceries

Try to avoid canned or frozen foods (besides ice cream) and processed foods. Do not overstock because most fresh foods will perish. Look out for coupons and sales! Your shopping list will depend on your meal plans.
